About the coffee shop

Our coffee shop is located in the Twin Peaks Library. It features 17 tables and free Wi-Fi.

Our main mission is keeping guests cozy and energized.

Besides that, we try to reduce our environmental impact — so we don't sell single-use cups. But you can buy one of our reusable cups or bring your own mug!
